URL format for one station & one date:"http://127.0.0.1:5001/api/v1/station/date/"   Example:"http://127.0.0.1:5000/api/v1/12/1955-10-21/"

URL format for one station & all dates:"http://127.0.0.1:5001/api/v1/"  Example: "http://127.0.0.1:5000/api/v1/10/"

URL format for one station & one year:"http://127.0.0.1:5001/api/v1/"   Example: "http://127.0.0.1:5000/api/v1/yearly/10/1986/"
